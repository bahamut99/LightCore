What is LightCore?

Your Bio-Digital Twin

LightCore is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you understand the connections between your daily activities, your sleep, and your overall well-being. By keeping a brief daily log, you provide the app with the data it needs to become your "bio-digital twin," reflecting your body's patterns and rhythms over time.

How to Use LightCore: A Quick Guide

1. The Daily Log & Sleep Data (Center & Right Columns)

This is your main daily input. In the large text box, write a few sentences about your day. What did you do? How did you feel? What did you eat? The more detail, the better the insights will be. On the right, add the hours you slept and rate the quality of your sleep from 1 (poor) to 5 (excellent).

2. AI Analysis (Center Column)

After you submit your log, our AI gets to work. It reads your entry and provides three key scores: Mental Clarity, Immune Risk, and Physical Output. It also provides brief notes explaining its reasoning. This gives you an instant snapshot of your readiness for the day.

3. Trend Charts (Left Column)

The charts on the left visualize your scores over the last 7 or 30 days. This is where you can see patterns at a glance. Are you trending up or down? Did a few nights of poor sleep impact your mental clarity? The charts make these connections obvious.

4. Recent Entries (Center Column)

This table shows a history of your recent logs. For a quick overview, you can see the scores for each day. If you want to see the full details of a specific entry‚Äîincluding your original log text and the AI's notes‚Äîsimply click on the row to open the full view.

5. AI Insight & History (Right Column)

The "AI Insight" card is where LightCore truly shines. After you've logged a few entries, the AI will start to find deeper correlations in your data. It might notice that your physical output is always higher after a good night's sleep, or that your mental clarity dips when you mention feeling stressed. These insights are saved in the "Insight History" so you can review them anytime.
