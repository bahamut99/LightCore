Privacy Policy for LightCore

Last Updated: August 17, 2025

Summary

LightCore helps you build a personal wellness “bio digital twin.” You can choose between a classic dashboard and a 3D Neural-Cortex view—both show the same underlying data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

  • Account: Sign-in is via Supabase. We don’t see your password.
  • Your logs: Your text plus derived scores (clarity, immune, physical readiness). Optional “events” may be extracted (e.g., “Workout @ 7:30am”).
  • Settings: Your UI preference (Neural-Cortex or Classic) is saved to your profile.
  • Google Health (optional): If you connect, we store OAuth tokens only to fetch today’s step count. We do not store historical steps.
  • Security: Database rows are protected with Row-Level Security (RLS) so you only access your own data.
  • Control: You can export or delete your data. You can disconnect Google Health at any time.

What We Collect

  • Account info: Email address. Authentication is managed by Supabase; passwords aren’t visible to us.
  • Profile: Preferred UI (neural / classic), timestamps.
  • Daily logs: Text you enter and AI-derived fields (clarity/immune/physical scores, notes, tags).
  • Events (optional): Structured items parsed from a log (e.g., “Caffeine 9:10am”).
  • Nudges: Insight cards generated by your data and whether you acknowledged them.
  • Google Health (optional): OAuth access/refresh tokens and expiry, limited to the step count scope.
  • Device data: Your browser’s IANA timezone so “today” matches your local day.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Run the App: To authenticate you, show your logs, trends, and generate AI guidance.
  • For Personalization: To honor your chosen dashboard (Classic vs Neural-Cortex).
  • For Health Integration (optional): To fetch your current day's step count from Google Fit using your local timezone.
  • For Security: To keep the service secure and prevent unauthorized access using RLS and HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Our database & authentication provider with Row-Level Security.
  • Netlify: Our hosting & serverless functions provider.
  • Google (Gemini & Fit APIs): Used for AI analysis and optionally for step counts. Your Google tokens are used only to read today’s steps and can be revoked at any time.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Export Data: We plan to add an in-app control to download your data.
  • Delete Account: We plan to add an in-app control to remove your account and data.
  • Disconnect Google Health: Toggle off in "Connected Services" or revoke access in your Google Account settings.
  • Change UI Preference: Switch anytime on the Settings page.
