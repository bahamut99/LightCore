Privacy Policy

Effective: 17 Aug 2025

LightCore helps you build a personal wellness “bio digital twin.” You can choose between a classic dashboard and a 3D Neural-Cortex view—both show the same underlying data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

We don’t sell your data. We store the minimum needed to run the app and give you in-app controls to export and delete your data.

Summary

  • Account: We use Supabase for sign-in. We don’t see your password (it’s handled by Supabase).
  • Your logs: Text you enter plus derived scores (clarity, immune, physical readiness). Optional “events” may be extracted from a log (e.g., “Workout @ 7:30am”).
  • Settings: Your UI preference (Neural-Cortex or Classic) is saved to your profile and also in local storage so it feels “sticky.”
  • Google Health (optional): If you connect, we store the Google OAuth tokens only to fetch today’s step count. We do not store historical steps; we read the current day on demand and show it.
  • Security: Database rows are protected with Row-Level Security (RLS) so you only ever access your own data.
  • Control: You can export or delete your data from inside the app. Disconnect Google Health at any time.

What we collect

  • Account info: Email address. Authentication is managed by Supabase; passwords are not visible to us.
  • Profile: Preferred UI (neural or classic), timestamps.
  • Daily logs: Text you enter and AI-derived fields such as clarity/immune/physical scores and notes.
  • Events (optional): Structured items parsed from a log (e.g., “Caffeine 9:10am”).
  • Nudges: Insight cards generated by your data; whether you acknowledged them.
  • Google Health (optional): OAuth access + refresh tokens and expiry, limited scope to step counts; current day’s step total displayed on request.
  • Device data: Your browser’s IANA timezone (e.g., “America/Chicago”) so “today” steps match your local day.
  • Local storage: A small key to remember your UI preference and support smoother routing.

How we use it

  • Run the app: Authenticate you, show your logs, trends, and guidance.
  • Personalization: Honor your chosen dashboard (Classic vs Neural-Cortex).
  • Health integration (optional): Fetch your current day step count from Google Fit in your local timezone.
  • Quality & safety: Keep the service secure (e.g., prevent unauthorized access via RLS).
  • Support: Help troubleshoot if you contact us.

Third-party services

  • Supabase (database & auth) — stores your data and enforces Row-Level Security.
  • Netlify (hosting & serverless functions) — runs the backend endpoints we call from the app.
  • Google Fit (optional) — provides your step counts when you connect Google Health.

These providers process your data on our behalf and transmit it over HTTPS. Your Google tokens are used only to read today’s steps and can be revoked at any time.

Google Health details

  • Scope: We request the minimal Google Fit scope to read step counts (com.google.step_count.delta).
  • Tokens: We store access/refresh tokens and their expiry in your user_integrations row to call Google’s API; if expired, we refresh them (when configured).
  • No step history stored: We do not persist daily step history in our database; the current day’s total is fetched and shown to you.
  • Timezone: The step window (start/end of “today”) is computed from your device’s IANA timezone.
  • Disconnect: Toggle off in “Connected Services” to delete the integration row; you can also revoke access in your Google Account settings.

Security

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Each query is scoped so you can only read or change your own rows.
  • Transport & storage: Data is sent over HTTPS. Our providers encrypt data at rest.
  • Least privilege: The app requests only the permissions required to function.

No system can be 100% secure, but we design LightCore to minimize data collection and exposure.

Your choices & rights

  • Export: Use the in-app Export my data control to download a copy of your data.
  • Delete: Use the in-app Delete account control to remove your account and associated data.
  • Disconnect Google Health: Toggle off in “Connected Services,” or revoke access from your Google Account.
  • Change UI preference: Switch at any time; your preference is saved to your profile.
  • Contact: Email us if you need help exercising these options.

Data retention

  • Account & logs: Kept while your account is active. Deleted when you use the in-app delete control.
  • Google tokens: Stored only while you’re connected; removed when you disconnect or delete your account.
  • Local storage: UI preference can be cleared by your browser or by switching views.

International transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States or other regions where they operate data centers. We rely on their security and compliance programs and use standard contractual protections where applicable.

Children’s privacy

LightCore isn’t directed to children. If you believe a child has used LightCore, please contact us so we can remove their data.

Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page if our practices change. When we make material changes, we’ll adjust the effective date above. If needed, we’ll provide additional notice in-app.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at support@yourdomain.com.
