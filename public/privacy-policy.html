Privacy Policy

Effective: 17 Aug 2025

LightCore helps you build a personal wellness “bio digital twin.” You can choose between a classic dashboard and a 3D Neural-Cortex view—both show the same underlying data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

We don’t sell your data. We store the minimum needed to run the app and give you in-app controls to export and delete your data.

Summary

  • Account: Sign-in is via Supabase. We don’t see your password.
  • Your logs: Your text plus derived scores (clarity, immune, physical readiness). Optional “events” may be extracted (e.g., “Workout @ 7:30am”).
  • Settings: Your UI preference (Neural-Cortex or Classic) is saved to your profile and also in local storage so it feels “sticky.”
  • Google Health (optional): If you connect, we store OAuth tokens only to fetch today’s step count. We do not store historical steps.
  • Security: Database rows are protected with Row-Level Security (RLS) so you only access your own data.
  • Control: Export or delete your data in-app. Disconnect Google Health any time.

What we collect

  • Account info: Email address. Authentication is managed by Supabase; passwords aren’t visible to us.
  • Profile: Preferred UI (neural / classic), timestamps.
  • Daily logs: Text you enter and AI-derived fields (clarity/immune/physical scores, notes).
  • Events (optional): Structured items parsed from a log (e.g., “Caffeine 9:10am”).
  • Nudges: Insight cards generated by your data; whether you acknowledged them.
  • Google Health (optional): OAuth access/refresh tokens and expiry, limited scope to step counts; current day’s steps displayed on request.
  • Device data: Your browser’s IANA timezone so “today” matches your local day.
  • Local storage: Small key for UI preference & smoother routing.

How we use it

  • Run the app: Authenticate you, show your logs, trends, and guidance.
  • Personalization: Honor your chosen dashboard (Classic vs Neural-Cortex).
  • Health integration (optional): Fetch your current day step count from Google Fit using your local timezone.
  • Quality & safety: Keep the service secure and prevent unauthorized access (RLS + HTTPS).
  • Support: Help troubleshoot when you contact us.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database & authentication with Row-Level Security.
  • Netlify — hosting & serverless functions (our backend endpoints).
  • Google Fit (optional) — provides your step counts when you connect Google Health.

These providers process your data on our behalf and transmit it over HTTPS. Your Google tokens are used only to read today’s steps and can be revoked at any time.

Google Health details

  • Scope: Minimal Google Fit scope for steps (com.google.step_count.delta).
  • Tokens: Access/refresh tokens & expiry stored in your user_integrations row; refresh on expiry (when configured).
  • No step history stored: We don’t persist historical step totals; we fetch and show the current day only.
  • Timezone: “Today” uses your device’s IANA timezone for start/end of day.
  • Disconnect: Toggle off in Connected Services or revoke in your Google Account.

Security

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Queries are scoped so you can only read or change your own rows.
  • Transport & storage: Data is sent over HTTPS. Our providers encrypt data at rest.
  • Least privilege: We request only the permissions required to function.

No system is 100% secure, but we minimize data collection and exposure.

Your choices & rights

  • Export: Use the in-app Export my data control to download your data.
  • Delete: Use the in-app Delete account control to remove your account and data.
  • Disconnect Google Health: Toggle off in Connected Services or revoke in Google Account.
  • Change UI preference: Switch anytime; your choice is saved to your profile.
  • Contact: Email us if you need help exercising these options.

Data retention

  • Account & logs: Kept while your account is active. Deleted via the in-app delete control.
  • Google tokens: Stored while connected; removed when you disconnect or delete your account.
  • Local storage: UI preference may be cleared by your browser or when switching views.

International transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States or other regions where they operate data centers. We rely on their security and compliance programs and use standard contractual protections where applicable.

Children’s privacy

LightCore isn’t directed to children. If you believe a child has used LightCore, please contact us to remove their data.

Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page if our practices change. When we make material changes, we’ll adjust the effective date above and, if needed, provide in-app notice.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at support@yourdomain.com.
