Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 Aug 2025

LightCore helps you build a personal wellness “bio digital twin.” You can choose between a classic dashboard and a 3D Neural-Cortex view—both show the same underlying data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

We don’t sell your data. We store the minimum needed to run the app and give you in-app controls to export and delete your data.

Summary

  • Account: Sign-in is handled by Supabase. We don’t see your password.
  • Your logs: Text you enter + derived scores (clarity, immune, physical readiness). Optional “events” may be extracted from a log (e.g., “Workout @ 7:30 AM”).
  • Settings: Your UI preference (neural or classic) is saved to your profile and also in local storage so it feels “sticky.”
  • Google Health (optional): If you connect, we store Google OAuth tokens only to fetch today’s step count. We don’t store historical step data; we read the current day on demand.
  • Security: Your rows are protected with Row-Level Security (RLS). You can only access your own data.
  • Control: You can export or delete your data from inside the app. You can disconnect Google Health at any time.

What we collect

  • Account info: Email address for your LightCore account. Authentication is managed by Supabase; passwords are not visible to us.
  • Profile: Preferred UI (neural / classic), timestamps, minimal app settings.
  • Daily logs: Text you enter plus AI-derived fields such as clarity/immune/physical scores and LightCore notes.
  • Events (optional): Structured items parsed from a log (e.g., “Caffeine 9:10 AM”).
  • Nudges: Insight cards generated from your data, including whether you acknowledged them.
  • Google Health (optional): OAuth access/refresh tokens and expiry, scoped only for step counts; we fetch the current day’s steps to display to you.
  • Device data: Your browser’s IANA timezone (e.g., “America/Chicago”) so “today” matches your local day when fetching steps.
  • Local storage: A small key to remember your UI preference and help with fast routing.

How we use it

  • Run the app: Authenticate you, show your logs, trends, and guidance.
  • Personalization: Honor your chosen dashboard (Classic vs Neural-Cortex).
  • Health integration (optional): Fetch your current day step count from Google Fit using your local timezone.
  • Quality & safety: Keep the service secure and prevent unauthorized access (RLS + HTTPS).
  • Support: Help troubleshoot when you contact us.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database & authentication with Row-Level Security.
  • Netlify — hosting & serverless functions (our backend endpoints).
  • Google Fit (optional) — provides step counts when you connect Google Health.

These providers process your data on our behalf and transmit it over HTTPS. Your Google tokens are used only to read today’s steps and can be revoked at any time.

Google Health details

  • Scope: Minimal Google Fit scope for step counts (com.google.step_count.delta).
  • Tokens: We store access/refresh tokens and expiry in your user_integrations row to call Google’s API; if expired, we refresh them (when configured).
  • No step history stored: We don’t persist daily step history in our database; the current day’s total is fetched on demand.
  • Timezone: The “today” window is computed from your device’s IANA timezone.
  • Disconnect: Toggle off in “Connected Services” to delete the integration row; you can also revoke access in your Google Account settings.

Security

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Queries are scoped so you can only read or change your own rows.
  • Transport & storage: Data is sent over HTTPS. Our providers encrypt data at rest.
  • Least privilege: The app requests only the permissions required to function.

No system is 100% secure, but we minimize data collection and exposure.

Your choices & rights

  • Export: Use the in-app Export my data control to download your data.
  • Delete: Use the in-app Delete account control to remove your account and data.
  • Disconnect Google Health: Toggle off in “Connected Services,” or revoke access in your Google Account.
  • Change UI preference: Switch at any time; your choice is saved to your profile.
  • Contact: Email us if you need help exercising these options.

Data retention

  • Account & logs: Kept while your account is active. Deleted when you use the in-app delete control.
  • Google tokens: Stored only while connected; removed when you disconnect or delete your account.
  • Local storage: UI preference may be cleared by your browser or when switching views.

International transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States or other regions where they operate data centers. We rely on their security and compliance programs and use standard contractual protections where applicable.

Children’s privacy

LightCore isn’t directed to children. If you believe a child has used LightCore, please contact us so we can remove their data.

Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page if our practices change. When we make material changes, we’ll adjust the effective date above and, if needed, provide in-app notice.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at support@yourdomain.com.
